Flight Plan Converter

A simple tool to convert between various flight plan formats. For example, useful for converting .FPL files from SkyVector to .FMS or .PLN files for your simulator.

Having issues or want to suggest a feature? Send a direct message to Kognise#6356 on Discord. Note that this does not support airways yet. Instead they'll just show up as direct waypoints, which shouldn't be an issue in most simulators.
